Stockholm trip

Welcome to Stockholm. Stockholm is the capital and most populous city of Sweden. Stockholm is full of beautiful buildings. good foods and culture.

Stockholm travel guide

1. Don't bring a ton of cash. A lot of places accept card.

2. You must try Swedish foods. Swedish meat balls are the best.

3. Pack your warm clothes if you plan to visit Stockholm in the winter.

4. Have a conversation in Swedish using easy words. Say hello or goodbye with just 'hej' or 'hej hej' and 'tack' means thank.

5. Fika is the Swedish coffee and cake ritual. The traditional fika is with a cinnamon bun.

6. You can travel by train, metro or bus in Stockholm.
